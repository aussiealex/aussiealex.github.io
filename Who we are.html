Our history

After working many years in IT in various Government Departments, our founder wanted a change. He began a Masters Degree in Cyber Security at UniSA. This was a real eye opener. Cyber attacks are occuring non stop and the complexity and volume of them is increasing. The consensus now is for anyone connected to the internet; at home, at work, on a laptop, on a smart phone. Its not if you will be attacked but when

Hence Cyber Wary was born.
