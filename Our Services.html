Our Services

What We Offer

Securing an IT system is essential and can be daunting. That's why we're here to help

Initial vulnerability assessment

We will look at your system and analyse vulnerabilities and report our findings with advice on how to strengthen your security posture. There will be no obligation to hire us to implement the security controls, but we will give you a quote on what will be needed to do.

System Configuration

According to what we report, what your risk appetite is and budget we can implement security controls to make your system and data much more secure

Awareness Training

Social Engineering attacks lead to the largest amounts of successful attacks on individuals and small businesses. We can help you and your staff become much more aware of fraudulent emails and phone calls, and other types of attack.

Security Policy Development

To help with streamlining your IT security implementation, adaptation and maitenance we can write a security policy.

Business continuity plan Development

What if your business has a long term power outage or your premises is flooded or your IT system has been a victim of a ransomware attack? We will help you recover faster with a business continuity plan.
